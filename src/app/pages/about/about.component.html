About Us

Click2pass is a Canadian company owned by Canadians.

Our vision is to provide support to Canadians and other nationalities who would love to be Canadians themselves in the near future.

As of now we are providing support to help people require preparations for Canadian citizenship test and Ontario G1 driving test. We are committed to offer preparations for many other tests in the coming days.

We thrive to be a law abiding corporate citizen of Canada, and contribute to betterment of Canada, economically and socially. We truly believe in being a customer friendly company, hence welcome your comments and suggestions at all times.
