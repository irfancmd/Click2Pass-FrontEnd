How to Prepare for Ontario G1 Driving Test

Ontario G1 knowledge test is the hurdle to cross to obtain a G1 license which gradually leads to full G license. Ministry of Transportation (MTO) takes the test and it assess an individual's understanding of road signs and rules which contributes to their ability to safely drive a vehicle. The test asks 40 questions of which 20 are on road signs and the other 20 are on rules of the road. 16 questions from each section must be correctly answered to pass the test. There is no time limit for the test. Here are a few tips for G1 test preparations.

  • Questions are based on official MTO Driver's Handbook. Please study the book thoroughly to learn about road signs and rules of road.
  • Do not just memorize, rather try to understand the concepts behind each rule by studying the official book properly.
  • Various road signs have specific colors, hence give special attention to those.
  • Some questions can be quite tricky. So read it a few times and choose your answer using your knowledge and judgement.
  • Discuss the complex road signs and rules with your driving instructor or people who have knowledge of those.
  • There are many youtube videos on G1 driving test which might be helpful to enhance your knowledge.
  • Spend enough time at click2pass.ca to go through all the tests to gain expertise and confidence for Ontario G1 exam.
